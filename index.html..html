Privacy Policy

👋 Introduction

Covenant Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Covenant Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

By using Covenant, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name and email address when you register.
  • Habit data: The habits and progress you track in the app.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system, used to improve app performance.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app, collected anonymously via Firebase Analytics.

Guest users do not create an account — their data is stored locally on the device only.

☁️ Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase to provide core app functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication — secure sign-in and account management.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your habits and syncs them across your devices.
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Free users see advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show relevant ads, including:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data

Premium users do not see any advertisements.

AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

🔒 How We Use Your Data

We use your information only to:

  • Provide and improve the Covenant app experience.
  • Sync your habit data across your devices.
  • Send reminders you have set up yourself.
  • Process referral codes and Premium subscriptions.
  • Show relevant advertisements to free-tier users.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

🗄️ Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

Local data (for guest users) is stored on your device using Hive, a secure local database.

👶 Children's Privacy

Covenant is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of analytics by adjusting your device settings.
  • Limit ad personalization via your device's ad settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.
